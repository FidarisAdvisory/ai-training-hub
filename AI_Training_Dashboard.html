‚úÖ Strong Prompt

The Role-Context-Task Framework

‚è±Ô∏è 20 minutes üõ†Ô∏è Any AI Tool

The Framework:

  1. Role: Define who the AI should be
  2. Context: Provide necessary background
  3. Task: Specify exactly what you want

üéØ Your Challenge:

Create prompts for each scenario using RCT:

Scenario A: Explain a technical concept
[Role] Act as a technical communications specialist who excels at making complex topics accessible. [Context] I need to explain [CONCEPT] to [AUDIENCE] who have [BACKGROUND]. [Task] Create an explanation that uses analogies, avoids jargon, and takes no more than 2 minutes to read.
Scenario B: Get feedback on writing
[Role] Act as a senior editor at a business publication. [Context] I've written a [TYPE] for [AUDIENCE]. The goal is [OBJECTIVE]. [Task] Review and provide: (1) Overall assessment, (2) Three specific improvements, (3) Any unclear sentences.

üìã Steps to Complete:

  1. Choose one scenario above
  2. Fill in the template with a real situation
  3. Submit to an AI tool and evaluate
  4. Refine based on what was missing
  5. Compare the refined output to original

Iterative Refinement

‚è±Ô∏è 25 minutes üõ†Ô∏è ChatGPT or Claude

üí° Key Insight:

Your first prompt rarely produces perfection. The real skill is guiding the AI through follow-up prompts.

üîÑ Refinement Strategies:

StrategyExample Phrases
Adjust scope"Make this more concise" / "Expand point #2"
Change tone"More formal" / "Add warmth"
Add constraints"Limit to 200 words" / "Remove jargon"
Format change"Convert to bullets" / "Present as table"
Focus attention"Paragraph 2 is good‚Äîmatch the rest to it"

üéØ Your Challenge:

  1. Start: "Write an email announcing a new project management tool we're adopting."
  2. Refine 1: "Make it more enthusiastic without being cheesy. Add a specific example of time saved."
  3. Refine 2: "Add: (1) when training is available, (2) who to contact with questions, (3) deadline for setup."
  4. Refine 3: "Perfect. Now shorten by 30% while keeping all key information."
üí° Pro Tip
Save your successful refinement sequences. They become templates for similar future tasks.

Chain-of-Thought Prompting

‚è±Ô∏è 20 minutes üõ†Ô∏è Claude (best) or ChatGPT

üß† What is Chain-of-Thought?

Asking the AI to show its reasoning step-by-step before giving a final answer. This produces more accurate, thoughtful responses for complex problems.

Magic Phrases:

  • "Think through this step by step before answering."
  • "Explain your reasoning as you work through this."
  • "Before giving your recommendation, list the factors you're considering."
Without Chain-of-Thought
Should we expand into the European market?
With Chain-of-Thought
I'm considering whether our company should expand into the European market. Before giving a recommendation, please: 1. List the key factors that determine international expansion success 2. Identify what information you would need 3. Discuss major risks and mitigations 4. Then provide your recommendation with reasoning Think through this systematically.

üéØ Your Challenge:

  1. Choose a complex decision you need help with
  2. Ask the question directly (without CoT)
  3. Note the response quality
  4. Reframe with chain-of-thought instructions
  5. Compare depth, analysis quality, and reasoning

üß† META-PROMPTS

The most powerful advanced technique: prompts that create other prompts. Master this and you'll never struggle with prompting again.

üí°

What is a Meta-Prompt?

A meta-prompt is a prompt that generates other prompts. Instead of writing the perfect prompt yourself, you ask the AI to help create it.

The Concept:

  • Regular prompt: "Write a marketing email for our new product."
  • Meta-prompt: "Help me write the perfect prompt for generating a marketing email. Ask me the questions you need to create an effective prompt."
Simple Meta-Prompt Example
I want to write a prompt that will help me create effective cold outreach emails. Before I write this prompt, I need your help making it as effective as possible. Please: 1. Ask me 5-7 questions about my specific situation, audience, and goals 2. Based on my answers, write an optimized prompt I can use repeatedly 3. Explain why you included each element in the prompt

The AI will ask clarifying questions, and once you answer, it constructs a detailed, customized prompt for your specific needs.

‚ö°

Why Meta-Prompts Matter

BenefitWhy It Helps
Better prompts than you'd write aloneThe AI knows what information it needs to perform well
Discover blind spotsAI questions reveal context you didn't think to include
Reusable templatesCreate prompts you can use repeatedly with minor adjustments
Learn promptingSee what the AI includes to learn what makes prompts effective
Save timeA few minutes creating a meta-prompt saves hours of iteration

When to Use Meta-Prompts:

  • You'll use the prompt multiple times (templates, recurring tasks)
  • The task is complex and you're unsure what context to provide
  • You want to maximize quality for an important deliverable
  • You're learning what makes prompts effective for a new task type
üîë

KEY INSIGHT: Use the AI to Create Its Own Meta-Prompt

‚ö° The Power Move
You can use the SAME AI tool to create the meta-prompt itself. The AI knows what information it needs to perform well!

If you're not sure how to write a meta-prompt, just ask:

Level 1 - Basic Request
I frequently need to write [TYPE OF CONTENT]. Create a meta-prompt I can use to generate optimized prompts for this type of task. The meta-prompt should ask me all the right questions to produce excellent results.
Level 2 - More Specific
I'm a [ROLE] who regularly needs to [TASK]. I want to create a reusable meta-prompt that will help me generate great prompts for this task every time. Create a meta-prompt that: 1. Asks the essential questions needed for this type of task 2. Includes instructions for how you should format the final prompt 3. Can be used repeatedly with different projects Make the meta-prompt comprehensive but not overwhelming.

üîÑ The Self-Referential Loop:

  1. You ask the AI to create a meta-prompt
  2. The AI creates a prompt template with questions
  3. You fill in the template for your specific task
  4. The AI generates the perfect task-specific prompt
  5. You use that prompt to get your final output

Meta-Prompt Templates

üéØ Ready-to-use templates

Template 1: Content Creation

I need to create [TYPE OF CONTENT]. Before writing a prompt, please ask me about: 1. Target audience and their knowledge level 2. Primary goal (inform, persuade, entertain, etc.) 3. Key points that must be covered 4. Tone and style preferences 5. Length and format requirements 6. Examples of content I like 7. SEO keywords (if applicable) 8. Call-to-action needed After I answer, create a comprehensive prompt that will generate high-quality content meeting all specifications.

Template 2: Analysis

I need help analyzing [TOPIC/DATA]. Before creating the analysis prompt, ask me about: 1. What question am I trying to answer? 2. What data or information do I have? 3. Who will use this analysis and for what? 4. What format should the analysis take? 5. Specific frameworks or methodologies to use? 6. What would make this analysis actionable? 7. Deadline and depth required? Then create a prompt for thorough, useful analysis.

Template 3: Problem-Solving

I'm trying to solve [PROBLEM/MAKE DECISION]. Before creating a prompt, ask about: 1. What's the specific problem or decision? 2. What have I already tried or considered? 3. What constraints do I have? 4. What would an ideal outcome look like? 5. Who else is affected? 6. Timeline for resolution? 7. What information am I missing? Then create a prompt that helps me think through this systematically.

Create Your First Meta-Prompt

üéØ Your Challenge:

  1. Think of a task you do repeatedly (weekly reports, meeting agendas, status updates)
